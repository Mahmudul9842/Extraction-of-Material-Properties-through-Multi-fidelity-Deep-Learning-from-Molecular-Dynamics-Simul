Different percentages of HF data have been uniformly sampled from the whole sample space.

The naming of the high fidelity data files are done as:

SYSTEM PROPERTY_high_fidelity_PERCENTAGE OF HF DATA.txt

The naming of corresponding sample space files are done as:

T_rho_high_fidelity_PERCENTAGE OF HF DATA.txt

All the files containing in this folder are for one component system
